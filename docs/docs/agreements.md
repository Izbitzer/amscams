# Agreements

Operators and Builders of Mike Hankey's ALLSKY7 devices must agree to the terms of use and license agreements. 

## Available Documents 

* [Network Agreement](#) - Mutual understanding relating to the operation of ALLSKY7 devices, sharing of data, support and acceptable use.
* [Meteorite Working Group Agreement](#) - Written to address all aspects of meteorite recovery as a camera operator or member of the ALLSKY7 network 
* [Software Community License Agreement](#) - All users of the ALLSKY7 hardware or software must agree to terms of the community license or purchase a commerical use license for the device.
